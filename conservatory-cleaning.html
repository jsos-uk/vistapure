The Problem

Why is my roof turning green?

Conservatory roofs bear the brunt of the UK weather. The combination of rain, limited direct sunlight in winter, and falling leaves creates the perfect breeding ground for algae, lichen, and moss.

If left uncleaned, this organic growth doesn't just block out the sun and look unsightly from the bedroom windows—it can permanently stain UPVC, degrade rubber seals causing leaks, and permanently etch into polycarbonate panels.

Our Methodology

How we clean it safely.

We never use high-pressure washers, which can easily blow seals and cause catastrophic interior leaks. Here is our gentle, proven approach:

1

Pre-Assessment

We carefully inspect the roof from the ground to identify the material (glass or poly) and spot any existing loose seals or vulnerable areas.

2

Application

If the algae is particularly thick, we apply an environmentally friendly, specialist degreaser to break down the organic matter.

3

Gentle Agitation

Using long-reach carbon fiber poles and bespoke soft-bristled brush heads, we gently scrub the panels, lifting the dirt without scratching.

4

Pure Rinse & Detail

We flush the roof with 100% pure water, washing the dirt into the gutters. We finish by cleaning the finials, crestings, and exterior gutters.
