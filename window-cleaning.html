The Problem

Why traditional window cleaning fails.

For decades, window cleaners have used ladders, buckets of soapy water, and a squeegee. While the glass looks clean initially, the microscopic soapy film left behind acts like a magnet for dust and dirt the moment the cleaner leaves.

Furthermore, balancing on ladders means frames and sills are often neglected, leaving the actual structure of your windows dirty and prone to degrading.

Our Methodology

The Reach & Wash System.

The safest and most effective way to clean windows in the 21st century.

1

Purification

We process tap water through multi-stage reverse osmosis filters in our vans, reducing the TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) to exactly 000 parts per million.

2

Elevation

We pump the pure water up lightweight telescopic carbon-fibre poles, allowing us to safely reach windows up to 3 storeys high without using a single ladder.

3

Agitation

Using purpose-built soft-bristled brush heads, we gently scrub the glass, frames, and sills, breaking the bond between the dirt and your property.

4

Natural Dry

We give the window a final rinse with pure water and leave it to dry naturally. Because the water contains zero impurities, it dries perfectly clear.
