Our Technology

The Pure Water Difference

We don't use ladders, buckets, or soapy water. Our advanced reach and wash system provides a safer, deeper, and longer-lasting clean.

1. Water Purification

Regular tap water contains minerals that leave spots. We filter our water through a strict reverse osmosis process to remove 100% of impurities.

2. Reach & Wash

Using telescopic carbon fiber poles, we pump the pure water up to specialized brush heads, safely scrubbing away dirt from the safety of the ground.

3. Natural Drying

Because the water is entirely pure, we don't need to squeegee it dry. We rinse the window and let it dry naturally, leaving zero streaks or residue behind.
