Our Methodology

How we restore UPVC safely.

Scrubbing UPVC with harsh abrasive sponges or high pressure will ruin the glossy finish. Here is our gentle but highly effective approach:

1

Pre-Soak

We coat the fascias and soffits with a specialist, environmentally friendly degreaser to start breaking down the organic algae and black exhaust stains.

2

Dwell Time

We leave the solution to dwell for 5-10 minutes. This is crucial for loosening the dirt from the microscopic pores in the UPVC plastic without harsh scrubbing.

3

Agitation

Using long-reach carbon fiber poles and bespoke soft-bristled brushes, we vigorously agitate the plastic to lift the dissolved grime away from the surface.

4

Pure Water Rinse

Finally, we flush the entire roofline with 100% pure, deionized water. This washes all the dirt away and ensures the plastic dries completely spotless.
